# Product: Noor Al-Quloob Academy Landing Page

## Overview
Single-page landing site for **أكاديمية نور القلوب** (Noor Al-Quloob Academy) - a Quran and Islamic sciences academy serving women and children.

## Core Product Requirements

### Language & Localization
- **Primary Language**: Arabic only
- **Text Direction**: RTL (right-to-left) must be enforced throughout
- **Audience**: Women and children, including non-Arabic speakers learning Quran

### Design Standards
- **Quality Target**: Awwwards-submittable - prioritize visual excellence, smooth animations, and modern aesthetics
- **Visual Style**: Clean, elegant, culturally appropriate for Islamic educational content
- **Responsiveness**: Mobile-first approach essential for target demographic

### Content Structure
The landing page consists of exactly 5 sections in this order:

1. **Hero Section**: Opening statement, academy name, primary CTA
2. **Video Presentation**: Embedded video showcasing the academy
3. **Gallery**: Photo showcase using 18 images from `/gallery` folder (image1.jpeg - image18.jpeg)
4. **Founders Section**: Profiles of أ. فاتحة محمد and أ. وسيلة أم أيوب
5. **Footer**: Contact info, social links, copyright

### Educational Offerings
Free programs covering:
- Quran recitation and memorization
- Islamic sciences
- Family guidance
- Mental health support
- Prophetic biography (Seerah)

## Brand Voice & Values
- **Welcoming**: Emphasize accessibility and free education
- **Professional**: Maintain educational credibility
- **Inclusive**: Serve all ages and language backgrounds
- **Traditional yet Modern**: Classical Islamic knowledge with contemporary delivery

## Technical Constraints
- Use existing gallery images - do not generate or request new images
- Ensure all text content is in Arabic
- Implement proper RTL CSS/styling
- Optimize for performance (fast load times critical for target audience)
